Hey, I'm Tania!

Born and raised in Surabaya, I've spent my whole life exploring every corner of East Java — from the misty peaks of Mount Bromo to the secret warungs only locals know about.

Tourism isn't just my job, it's my passion! I believe the best travel experiences come from genuine connections and off-the-beaten-path discoveries. No boring bus tours here — just real adventures, real food, and real stories.

Whether you want to chase volcanic sunrises, dive into local culture, or create a completely custom adventure, I'm here to make your East Java dreams come true! Let's explore together 🌺
